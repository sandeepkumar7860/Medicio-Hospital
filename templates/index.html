    Cardiology

    Our cardiology department provides comprehensive heart care using advanced diagnostic tools and modern treatment techniques.

    We specialize in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of heart-related conditions including hypertension, coronary artery disease, and heart failure. Our expert cardiologists ensure personalized treatment plans for optimal heart health.

    Neurology

    Advanced neurological care focused on disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system.

    Our neurology specialists diagnose and treat conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, migraines, Parkinson’s disease, and other neurological disorders using cutting-edge technology and patient-centered care.

    Hepatology

    Specialized care for liver, gallbladder, and digestive system disorders.

    Our hepatology department provides expert diagnosis and treatment for liver diseases including hepatitis, fatty liver disease, cirrhosis, and other digestive health conditions.

    Pediatrics

    Compassionate healthcare services for infants, children, and adolescents.

    We provide preventive care, vaccinations, developmental monitoring, and treatment for childhood illnesses in a safe and child-friendly environment.

    Ophthalmologists

    Comprehensive eye care services for clear vision and long-term eye health.

    Our ophthalmology department offers vision testing, cataract treatment, glaucoma management, and advanced eye surgeries to ensure optimal eye health for all ages.
